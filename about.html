What is VacationSwap?
VacationSwap is a revolutionary tool that aims to cumbersome steps and research in planning a vacation. With user submitted itineraries called ‘walkthroughs’ and an in-depth rating and reviewing system, exploring the world has never been easier.
Who are the creators?
We (Udit Bhansali, Sohit Pal, Jimmy Stein, Michael Zurawski) are 4 college students who all major or minor in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts - Lowell.
Why did we make this website?
Vacations are fun and meant to be relaxing, why should the process of planning one be any different? We created this website to solve an issue that we have faced in our own lives and to complete a requirement for GUI Prototyping II with Professor Wenjin Zhou.
